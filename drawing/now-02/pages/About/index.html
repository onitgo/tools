Welcome to onitgo.com :

Welcome to onitgo.com! We are a team of four individuals with backgrounds in fine arts, specializing in drawing, handwriting, and calligraphy. We love sharing our passion for drawing and handwriting techniques with others through simple and easy methods. We live simply, and our motto is "Let's Learn" in a simple way.

Whether you're a beginner seeking to learn the basics or an experienced artist looking for new techniques, our goal is to provide short and simple video tutorials on drawing and handwriting that are easy to understand, with clear instructions and methods to unlock the creativity in each of us.

Meet our team :

Karvi: An aspiring caricaturist, handwriting tutor, and founder of onitgo.com, Karvi is deeply passionate about art in all its forms. With a background in fine arts and a love for teaching, Karvi is committed to making drawing and handwriting accessible to everyone through easy-to-follow video tutorials.

Ajay K: A talented architect and calligrapher, Ajay brings creativity and expertise to our tutorials. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for simplifying complex handwriting techniques, Ajay ensures that our tutorials are informative and enjoyable for learners of all levels.

Sai Kiran : The user-friendly interface of onitgo.com is designed and developed by Kiran. As a skilled software web developer and user interface designer, Kiran adds a polished look and feel, along with user-friendly interfaces, to our tutorials using the latest technologies.

Chandrika : A passionate female doodle artist and interior designer, Chandrika is on a mission to spread joy and creativity through playful illustrations. She adds simplified doodles and variety to our tutorials with her unique artistic style. With a passion for sharing knowledge and inspiring creativity, Chandrika strives to empower others to explore their artistic talents.

Why Choose Us?

Passion for Art: We are artists at heart, and our passion for art shines through in everything we do.

Expertise: As fine art students, we have a solid understanding of artistic principles and techniques.

Simplicity: We believe that art should be enjoyable and accessible to everyone, which is why we strive to present our tutorials in simple and easy-to-understand methods.

Join Our Community:

We invite you to join our growing community of drawing and handwriting enthusiasts! Whether you're looking to learn a new skill or simply enjoy exploring the world of art, we're here to support you every step of the way.
