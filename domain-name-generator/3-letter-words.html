Let's take an example: "ABC.com"

Three letter domain names, like "ABC.com," are very popular and hard to find. They are short, easy to remember, and usually already owned by someone. When they do become available, they are often sold at a very high price. For many businesses, startups, or individuals, buying a three-letter domain can be too expensive due to the high price set by the current owner. Because these short domains are in such high demand, they are difficult to get. However, there are other ways to find a unique and professional domain name. Let's take a look.
